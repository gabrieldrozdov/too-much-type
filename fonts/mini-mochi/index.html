Mini Mochi

MINI MOCHI is a playful, delectable, variable display font with a little bit of *stretch* and a little bit of *flavor*. Each letter is like a mochi-shaped cube engraved with pudgy counterforms that squish and mutate in response to two variable axes ("stretch" and "flavor"). While originally inspired by the anonymous tofu-shaped glyphs that appear when a font is missing a character, Mini Mochi evolved into something of its own. (On a side note, the flavorful glyphs also look kind of like rotten teeth. Don‘t eat too much sugar or you might end up like these letters!)
