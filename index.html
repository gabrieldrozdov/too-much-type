We make fonts! Here they are! They don’t bite!

Test and download our fonts from this menu. You can even add whatever you type to the desktop and play around with it there! Check out the Controls menu while you’re at it buddy.

Besides fonts, we also code type specimens exploring new technologies and use cases! Some of our work remixes other open-source fonts. For full credits, head over to the Info menu to see who made what!

Let’s play! Use these controls to move the letters and windows on the desktop around. You can also click on the letters to change their individual settings!

Want to add more letters? Head over to the Fonts menu and type out your desires! Once you’ve got all the letters you want, drag them around and print your creation!

Too Much Type designs and distributes experimental typefaces and type specimens. Every project is open-source, which means that you're free to use or edit our fonts and code however you like. Enjoy!

Credits

Code and fonts by No Replica / Gabriel Drozdov. Want to commission something? Reach out!

The Urging Osmosis fonts were originally designed as gifts for my RISD friends: Alec Figuracion, Berett Wilber, Claudia Morris, Emily Bluedorn, Kaela Kennedy, and Lydia Chodosh.

Paint is a revival of a font from Mario Paint.

Some of our fonts are remixed versions of other open-source fonts! Find the original fonts here:

  • AUTHENTIC Sans by Christina Janus and Desmond Wong
  • Avara by Raphaël Bastide
  • FT88 by Ange Degheest, Mandy Elbé, and Oriane Charvieux
  • Work Sans by Wei Huang
